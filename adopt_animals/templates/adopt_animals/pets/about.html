About

Adopt Animals is the web board for people who try to adopt pets and also helps people who want to adopt up their pets.
We're all about making fewer homeless pets and getting them into homes.

How to use?

For people who is looking for pets

If you are looking for pets, search to find pets for adoption on the top page!
Once you find animals, we don't intervene in any kinds of process
so please send a message to the pet owner for adoption and
talk about how to proceed adoption process

For people who is looking for foster homes

If you are looking look for foster homes,
go to the "New Post" page and create a post with precise infomations.
Once you recieve messages, please talk about how to proceed adoption process.
We don't intervene in any kind of process.
